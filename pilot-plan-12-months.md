# Pilot Plan — 12 Months

## Overview

This document outlines the planned milestones for the Cartagena POAP Route pilot. The exact timeline for each phase depends on partner approvals, logistical coordination with local stakeholders, and HOOP/Pulse integration scheduling. Milestones will be updated as dependencies are confirmed.

**Total pilot duration:** 12 months from grant award  
**Budget:** $45,000 USD  
**Implementing organization:** Cartagena Onchain

---

## Phase 1 — Foundation (Months 1–3)

**Goal:** Complete all design, partnership, and technical setup work required to launch.

### Milestones

| # | Milestone | Deliverable |
|---|-----------|-------------|
| 1.1 | Route design finalized | Written checkpoint map with 5 confirmed locations (mix of landmarks + vendors) |
| 1.2 | HOOP/Pulse integration confirmed | Technical setup complete; POAP events created in Pulse; QR generation tested |
| 1.3 | Vendor onboarding complete | ≥5 vendor/location partners onboarded using the vendor onboarding checklist |
| 1.4 | Vendor training delivered | All checkpoint staff trained on QR custody, participant assistance, and incident reporting |
| 1.5 | Redemption point confirmed | At least one official redemption point operational (tourism office or partner) |
| 1.6 | Physical reward designed and produced | Initial batch of completion rewards (e.g., collectible magnets) manufactured |
| 1.7 | Participant notice and privacy materials published | Notice text live at all claim points |
| 1.8 | Prospective partner discussions advanced | MOUs or letters of intent drafted where applicable (municipal, ecosystem partners) |

*Note: Timeline for 1.8 depends on third-party decision cycles. MOUs are not a prerequisite for pilot launch.*

---

## Phase 2 — Pilot Launch & First Activation (Months 3–6)

**Goal:** Launch the public route and achieve first significant participation data.

### Milestones

| # | Milestone | Deliverable |
|---|-----------|-------------|
| 2.1 | Public pilot launch | Route live; all 5 checkpoints operational |
| 2.2 | Launch activation event | At least one POAPthon walk or community event held |
| 2.3 | First participation data reported | Quarterly Report #1 submitted to UNICEF (aggregate data) |
| 2.4 | First reward redemptions processed | ≥25 physical rewards distributed |
| 2.5 | Anti-fraud controls validated | Rotating QR protocol confirmed operational; no material fraud incidents |

---

## Phase 3 — Iteration & Scale (Months 6–9)

**Goal:** Apply learnings from Phase 2, improve operations, and grow participation.

### Milestones

| # | Milestone | Deliverable |
|---|-----------|-------------|
| 3.1 | Mid-pilot review completed | Internal review of participation data, vendor feedback, incident log |
| 3.2 | Route or vendor adjustments (if needed) | Updated checkpoint map or vendor roster if performance warrants |
| 3.3 | Second activation event | At least one additional community event or tourism partnership activation |
| 3.4 | Mid-pilot progress report | Quarterly Report #2 submitted to UNICEF |
| 3.5 | Cumulative completions ≥75 | On-chain data confirms ≥75 wallets hold all 5 route POAPs |

---

## Phase 4 — Close-Out & Reporting (Months 9–12)

**Goal:** Complete the pilot, document all impact, and publish findings.

### Milestones

| # | Milestone | Deliverable |
|---|-----------|-------------|
| 4.1 | Route operations concluded | Official end date for checkpoint QR activation |
| 4.2 | Final participation targets met | ≥150 completed routes; ≥500 total POAP claims; ≥150 rewards redeemed |
| 4.3 | Final redemption event | Optional closing community event; remaining rewards distributed |
| 4.4 | Final impact report produced | Public report published to this GitHub repository |
| 4.5 | Final grant report submitted | Full financial and programmatic report submitted to UNICEF |
| 4.6 | Sustainability and replication brief | Document outlining lessons learned and recommendations for other cities |
